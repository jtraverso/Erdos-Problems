\subsection*{Statement audited}
$\tau_3^*(S_{p,q})=\tfrac{P+pq}{3}$ for $q\le p-1$, $=P$ for $q\ge p-1$ (with $P=\binom p2$), and $=0$ in the triangle-free corners $p\le1$ and $(p,q)=(2,0)$; and the induced values of $\Phi_\tau(S_{p,q})$.

\subsection*{Method}
For a grid of $(p,q)$ the value $\tau_3^*(S_{p,q})$ is computed by exact linear programming and compared against the closed form; $\Phi_\tau=|E|-2\tau_3^*$ is compared against Corollary 6.3. The degenerate case $S_{2,0}=K_2$ is checked explicitly.
