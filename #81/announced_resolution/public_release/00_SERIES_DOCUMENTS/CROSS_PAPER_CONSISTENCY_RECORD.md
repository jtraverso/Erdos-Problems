# Cross-Paper Consistency Record

The final package uses the same conventions across all three papers.

## Complete-split construction

The family

\[
K_p\vee\overline K_{2p}
\]

is treated consistently as the complete-split, indeed threshold, construction underlying the \(1/6\) scale.

## Quantitative hierarchy

- Paper I proves a direct fractional split bound and introduces the affine-profile method.
- Paper II gives the exact finite fractional chordal maximum.
- Paper III gives the integral split bound with linear error.

## Computational material

Across the series, computational checks are described only as:

- regression tests;
- exact verification evidence;
- reproducibility material.

They are not presented as proof premises.

## Open qualifications

The package does not assert:

- independent human peer review;
- definitive novelty clearance;
- a completed forward-citation search.
