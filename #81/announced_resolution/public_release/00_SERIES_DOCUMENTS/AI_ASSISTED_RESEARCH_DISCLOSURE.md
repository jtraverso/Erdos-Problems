# AI-Assisted Research and Review Disclosure

AI-assisted systems were used during exploratory, computational, adversarial, organizational, and editorial stages of this research program.

Their roles included:

- testing algebraic identities;
- generating and checking finite regression instances;
- identifying edge cases;
- organizing proof ledgers and claims matrices;
- assisting with manuscript editing and packaging.

The scripts included in this package are supplementary regression and reproducibility tools. They are not logical premises of any theorem.

No AI system is an author. All mathematical claims, citations, code, and editorial decisions remain the responsibility of Juan Pablo Traverso Gianini.

The internal and AI-assisted audits documented here do not constitute independent human peer review or journal refereeing.
